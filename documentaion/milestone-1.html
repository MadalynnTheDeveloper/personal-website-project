PWP Milestone Project 1

Purpose:

I am passionate about living a healthy long lifestyle while implementing a nutrition and fitness plan, letting those in need know I am here for you to be successful! Providing accountability to help encourage, engage, and equip those willing to believe in the possibilities of a healthy life style.

Audience:

I want to inspire positive change by being an important role model by teaching, evaluating and coaching personalized nutritional advice and facts to my community here in Albuquerque, New mexico at a low and affordable cost. Offered to all ages locally based on health goals or medical needs.

Goal:

The goal of this website is to allow my clients to have full access to personal tailored nutritional and fitness plans according to each individual for a better and healthy lifestyles. Providing clear and concise communication, nutritional facts to all clients maintaining accountability to make better dietary decisions and reminding they are not alone. They can find comfort in viewing my website for new scientific information to affect their decisions, and use reasoning to spot strengths and weaknesses as I walk along side their journey.

Persona

Name:

Anna Smith

Job:

Nutritionist and Gym Owner

Technology:

IPhone11 running IOS 13 and chrome on her mobile device a Macbook Pro 2017 running MacOs Catalina and primarily Using Chrome, Safari, and Edge.

Behaviors and Attitudes:

She has maintained a people-oriented business and enjoys working with diverse people. She is dedicated, a good listener who pays attention to clients goals and concerns and has an analytical mind advising clients on nutrition ideas and constantly looking for facts. She has proven to be an excellent problem solver applying her nutritional knowledge to craft eating plans for clients and patients. She is able to interpret scientific studies and apply nutrition science to offer useful food guidance. She has a gentle but tough approach to help clients cope with dietary problems, and has a natural sense and understands clients reactions to a plan or to new information. She has a personable and patient demeanor which has proven effective rapport and trust with clients.

Frustrations:

She becomes frustrated when clients lack complete honesty and attention when they are speaking on their health issues, dietary concerns and challenges. When clients are not truthful regarding behavior, lifestyle patterns and who are not willing to take the challenge and follow through when results are delayed. She also becomes frustrated when clients cancel weekly check-in last minute.

User Story:

As a business owner and active nutritionist I live out a daily healthy lifestyle by leading as an example. I have a good support system which helps me maintain my foundational skills equipping me to implement new ideas for future growth. I work independently building and maintaining my clientele. I work with tools and equipment including calorimeters to gauge metabolism, glucose meters to evaluate blood sugar and bioelectric impedance machines to electronically measure body fat to educate each client. I also work with analytical software programs and databases in providing scientific facts and implementing my way of teaching through a simple way of living a healthy lifestyle.
